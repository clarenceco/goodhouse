About Us

Goodhouse Industries, Inc. (GHII), established in 1992, is a Filipino company that specializes in the design and manufacture of standard and custom industrial air filters, cleanroom systems, dust collectors, HVAC equipment and components for a wide range of applications.

Who We Serve

GHII offers a broad range of clean room and HVAC components and services to help maintain a controlled production environment for manufacturing plants


  •    Food and Beverage

  • Pharmaceutical Industry

  • Semiconductor and Electronics

  • Paint Booth for Automotive Assembly Plant

  • Cement, Flour and Feed Mills and other dust generating plants
